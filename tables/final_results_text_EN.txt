In total, 450 TCGA-LUSC patients were included in the train/test survival analysis, with 315 patients in the training subset and 135 patients in the test subset. The original elastic-net survival model selected 3 features. Its concordance index was 0.656 in the training set and 0.578 in the test set. The hazard ratio for the high-risk versus low-risk group was 2.234 in the training set (p = 4.49e-06) and 1.448 in the test set (p = 0.174). Time-dependent ROC analysis of the original model showed AUC values of 0.693, 0.685, and 0.703 at 1, 3, and 5 years in the training subset, respectively, and 0.629, 0.515, and 0.489 in the test subset. The Cox-prefilter model included 50 selected genes and was evaluated in 469 patients. This model achieved a concordance index of 0.648, with a hazard ratio of 2.428 for the high-risk versus low-risk group (95% CI 1.811–3.257, p = 3.14e-09). The corresponding time-dependent AUC values for the Cox-prefilter model were 0.658, 0.696, and 0.732 at 1, 3, and 5 years, respectively. Overall, the Cox-prefilter model showed better prognostic separation and slightly improved discrimination compared with the original model.
